Projects and Works> Cutnread.com

Turkish news platform.

Every media company unnecessarily prolongs their content and adds unnecessary information to make money. For this reason, it is very difficult for people to access information. Cutnread is a project that has seen this problem and has a vision to solve it. Cutnread, which provides news away from click-bait and unnecessary information, not only performs SEO compliance, but also offers short content to users.
